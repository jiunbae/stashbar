Stashbar is a macOS menu bar utility for quickly finding and opening recent files from folders you check often.

Pin folders such as Screenshots, Downloads, or active project directories, then browse their latest contents from one compact popover. You can preview files with Quick Look and continue with common Finder-style actions without bringing Finder to the front first.

Key Features
• Pin multiple folders and switch between them quickly
• Icon, list, and hierarchy views with sorting options
• Quick Look previews and Finder-style keyboard shortcuts
• Drag and drop, copy/paste, and move to Trash support
• Disk cache and security bookmarks for fast access after relaunch
• Launch at login so it is always ready in the menu bar

Stashbar is designed as a focused menu bar tool that makes repeated file-checking workflows shorter and more natural.
